fix $B_U, B_V, B_W$ each as [[vector space basis]] of the [[set]] $U,V,W$, respectively  
then, for $S\circ T : U \rightarrow W$ we have that:  
  
$$M_{B_UB_W}(S\circ T)=M_{B_VB_W}(S).M_{B_UB_V}(T)$$
